## About Me

I help organisations turn AI and digital capability into measurable business outcomes, bridging the gap between what technology can do and what the business actually needs.

Over the years, I have worked as a Product Owner and Business Analyst across enterprise AI platforms, conversational AI systems, and data-driven digital products, predominantly in complex, cross-functional environments where stakeholder alignment and clear requirements were the difference between delivery and drift.

My work spans the full delivery lifecycle: from eliciting requirements and mapping processes in discovery workshops, through to writing user stories, defining acceptance criteria, managing backlogs, and monitoring product performance post-launch. I have hands-on experience evaluating Generative AI and LLM capabilities against real business problems, and translating that analysis into roadmap decisions stakeholders can act on.

Alongside industry delivery, I research the governance and regulatory dimensions of AI adoption, with a specific focus on EU AI Act compliance and GDPR in enterprise contexts, grounded in my MSc thesis at the University of Galway. This gives me an unusual ability to analyse not just what an AI system does, but whether it should do it, and how to document that decision for regulated environments.

📍 Based in Galway, Ireland · Stamp 1G (Full work authorisation) · Open to BA, PM, PO, and applied AI research roles

- **SQL reporting layer** tracking platform KPIs and performance benchmarks

### 🚚 [B2B Fleet Aggregator API](https://github.com/sandesh-s-hegde/b2b-fleet-aggregator-api)
> *Python · FastAPI · PostgreSQL · Neon · Render*

High-performance middleware bridging macro-strategic forecasting with micro-tactical fleet execution. Converts predictive insights into actionable logistics workflows.

- **Dynamic Surge Pricing:** Automatically adjusts supplier rates based on real-time fleet utilization and availability algorithms.
- **Aggregation Engine:** Consolidates multi-supplier capacity and prioritizes low-emission vehicle routing.
- **Enterprise Architecture:** Deployed across a multi-cloud environment (Render & Neon) with comprehensive CI/CD, global exception handling, and observability telemetry.

### 👁️ [Project Sentinel — Computer Vision for Logistics Visibility](https://github.com/sandesh-s-hegde/project-sentinel)
> *Python · YOLOv8 · Computer Vision · Flow Entropy*

Addressed the "Data Gap" in Logistics Service Provider (LSP) risk modelling by treating video feeds as a structured data source.

- **Engineered a YOLOv8 + Sparse Optical Flow pipeline** to track kinematic disorder in high-density distribution nodes.
- **Converted unstructured visual noise** into "Flow Entropy" metrics, enabling real-time volatility estimation.
- **Designed to function as the "Sensor" layer** for Empirically Grounded Analytics (EGA).
